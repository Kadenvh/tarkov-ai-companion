## 2. Wipes & prestige — CRITICAL

- **The game no longer wipes.** Nov 15, 2025 was the last reset. Mains persist indefinitely (PvP and PvE). No reset occurred Nov 2025 – Jul 2026 → **every main is ~8 months into permanent progression; "late-wipe" economy conditions.**
- **Seasons replace wipes** (PvP, opt-in): separate seasonal character at lvl 1, empty stash, PoE-style modifiers (debuffs unlock buffs), battlepass, seasonal tasks. Main untouched; rewards carry back at season end. Two/year, free. **Season 1 "Kord Breach" has NOT started as of 2026-07-11** (ships with 1.1.0, late July consensus; min ~74 days). Season 2 planned Q4 2026.
- **Prestige = the only voluntary reset & only ending-replay mechanism.** Reworked twice (1.0.2: 55→47; **1.0.5: tiers 1–4 with much lower bars — Kappa NO LONGER required**). Wiki: "New Beginning (Prestige 1)" starts at **level 25**; Prestige 4 requires level 40, P3, 20 PMC kills, 30 Rogue kills, transits, FIR hand-ins, ~15 Str/End. Resets: level→1, stash, hideout, **all task/story progress**. Keeps: achievements, prestige rank, Arena Armory, a chosen gear slice, portion of skills/mastery. Rewards: cosmetics, +1 daily & +1 weekly task slots, +1 Charisma per season start. ⚠ Many guide sites still describe the old "level 55 + Collector" model — stale; trust wiki quest pages + May 2026 coverage. ⚠ PvP-main applicability of prestige not 100% clear (sources emphasize PvE).

## 3. Kappa / Collector (current)

- **~257 kappa-required tasks** (kappaquests.com 2026-06-21; one app claims 261 — minor tracker disagreement).
- **Collector: unlocks at player level 45** (huge cut from pre-1.0 era 62; highest single gate in chain ~48 per some trackers).
- Hand-in items: **41 vs 43** disagreement across sources; wiki unfetchable — assume 41–43.
- Kappa is one-time permanent on mains (no wipes) and **no longer a prestige prerequisite** (since May 2026). ⚠ Boreas tasks likely NOT kappa-required (unverified).

## 4. Story chapters & endings

- **10th chapter added post-launch: "Boreas"** (1.0.5, May 2026) — unlocks Icebreaker; started via Intel Center L3 distress signal or Paradigm Shipping Poster → Mechanic; **branches based on previous story choices**. Launch 9 (Tour, Falling Skies, Batya, Accidental Witness, They Are Already Here, Blue Fire, The Ticket, The Unheard, The Labyrinth) remain accurate. ⚠ Whether Tour is formally classed a "story chapter" post-Boreas: unconfirmed.
- **4 endings unchanged: Savior, Survivor, Fallen, Debtor.** Savior = canon/hardest (all optional objectives + Lightkeeper trust); Survivor = same final choice minus grind; Debtor = refuse Kerman/pay Prapor; Fallen = betray LK or stall The Ticket. Key branches: Armored Case (Prapor vs keep), accept/refuse Kerman, evidence-gathering.
- **Ending choices are permanent per character. Replay = Prestige or full account reset only.** Story tasks reset **only via prestige**. Seasonal characters are separate profiles (story availability on seasonal: unverified).
- BSG published ending-completion stats Mar 2026 (ixbt.games).

## 5. Lightkeeper

Still relevant in 1.0.6. Unlock: **Network Provider chain + ~level 35 + Scav karma ≥ +1.0 (some say 2.0) + DSP Transmitter**. **New in 1.0: "The Ticket" chapter can grant the DSP early, shortcutting Network Provider** — model the story route as an alternative LK unlock. Savior ending requires LK trust → LK intersects the best ending.

## 6. Flea market / economy (1.0+)

- **Flea unlocks at 15 but is category-gated by level**: L25 assault/marksman rifles, backpacks, lights/lasers; L30 barter items, gear components, injectors, keycards; L35–40 top ammo. **Full flea ≈ level 40.** Changes acquisition strategy for 15–40 (trader barters + FIR matter much longer).
- **FIR still required to list on flea; FIR lost on death.** (The "FIR removal" experiment was May 2024, pre-1.0 — not current.)
- **Insurance: no confirmed change in 1.0.0–1.0.6; rework announced for 1.1.0** (model undisclosed). Seasonal modifiers can disable insurance on seasonal characters.
- **1.1.0 also brings trader rep/task rebalance + task unlock-order reshuffle** — route data partially invalidates when it lands. **TarCoins/Expansion Hub** (live ~Jul 10): earn-in-raid currency for stash expansions/cosmetics/PvE zone access — soft progression, not task-affecting.

## 7. Arena crossover

- **Sync is one-way Arena → main-game PvE only** (excluding regular tasks); PvP sync unreliable/bugged through 2026. Treat Arena as unreliable for main-PvP leveling.
- **Ref transfers**: Roubles, GP coins (~250/day cap), Lega Medals, crates → EFT (either profile); ~5% item fee, 10–15% rouble tax, reduced by Ref loyalty + Charisma. Nothing moves main → Arena. Arena Armory survives prestige.

- World-first 1.0 Kappa: **under 1 week** (Cocao, Nov 21, 2025). Pestily Kappa spreadsheet community-updated for 1.0 (ItzPyroGG). kappaquests.com dependency route updated Jun 2026.
