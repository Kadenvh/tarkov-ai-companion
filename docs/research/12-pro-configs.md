## Honest verdict: does any REAL pro share his oddities?

On the documented evidence — **no pro config I could verify corroborates a single one** of the flagged oddities:

- **ADS > hipfire:** LVNDMARK runs ADS **equal** to hipfire; meta says keep them equal. **No corroboration.**
- **Side-mouse lean (Q freed):** LVNDMARK leans on **Q/E keyboard**. Side-button lean *is* a legitimate pro technique in the community generally, but I could not tie it to LVNDMARK or Viibin specifically. **Not corroborated by these two.**
- **Negative Clarity (-34):** LVNDMARK uses **+82**; meta uses positive ~50. Negative Clarity contradicts everyone. **No corroboration.**
- **SSR Ultra:** LVNDMARK and meta both run SSR **Low/Off** (SSR is a visibility/FPS cost). **Contradicted.**
- **Chromatic aberration ON:** universally turned **Off** by pros and meta. **Contradicted.**
- **Music volume ≠ 0:** could not verify LVNDMARK's audio at all; community norm is music at 0 for footstep clarity. **Unverified, but norm is against it.**

Where he **agrees** with the pack: VSync Off (unanimous), TAA (unanimous), a hard FPS cap, and Reflex On (matches meta; the LVND aggregator value is stale pre-Reflex-era). His FOV 69 and optic-zoom 1.0 sit comfortably in meta range.

### Important caveats before acting on this
1. All LVNDMARK numbers are **aggregator, uncited, 2022-2023**. His *current* 1.0 config exists (2025-11-16 Twitch clip) but is **video-locked** — so "LVNDMARK contradicts you" is really "LVNDMARK's *old, uncited* config contradicts you." Do not present it as gospel.
2. **Viibin contributes zero verified data.** Any comparison against Viibin is impossible; the prior report's Viibin values were fabricated by aggregators.
3. Several oddities (negative Clarity, high Brightness+Sharpen) read like a **deliberate high-contrast visibility tune** — a coherent personal choice, not necessarily "wrong." The takeaway is that it is *idiosyncratic*, not pro-standard, rather than *broken*.
